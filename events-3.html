Other Plant Protection Workshops, Seminars and Conferences

IAPPS provides a service to advertise forthcoming plant protection related conferences, seminars and workshops that Institutional and Affiliate members are organising or are involved with.

If you wish to take advantage of this service please send details of the event to the Secretary General (Dr "Short" Heinrichs – eheinrichs2@unl.edu), including the Title, Objective and brief details of the program, the Date, Location, and organisers' contact details.

IAPPS Regional Seminars, Workshops, and Congresses

During the past 10 years members of the IAPPS Governing Board have worked with colleagues in various regional organisations to organise seminars and workshops on important plant protection issues. The list of past workshops and seminars can be viewed here.

With the onset of the Covid 19 virus epidemic, there are no immediate plans for regional seminars or workshops. Currently, the IAPPS Board is exploring future options for continuing these successful activities.

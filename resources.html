IAPPS Global Plant Protection News

Global Plant Protection News (GPPN) is a free news service provided by the International Association for the Plant Protection Sciences (IAPPS). Articles relevant to plant protection are posted on the WordPress platform and sent via email to subscribers as well as recent posts being listed on the IAPPS website. www.plantprotection.org

This online service provides IAPPS members and other visitors to our site with recent news articles, regarding all topics of relevance to plant protection and includes articles of interest to all plant protection disciplines

Established in 2011, more than 2,900 articles have been posted to date. You can access recent postings or browse for articles here according to the categories list.
